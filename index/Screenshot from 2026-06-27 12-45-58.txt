Chemis an abbreviation or prefix for chemistry, chemical, or
chemist. © eriam-webster +1

Depending on the context, it generally refers to:

+ Chemistry: The scientific study of matter, its properties, composition, and how
substances interact and change.

* Chemical: A distinct compound or substance, particularly in manufacturing or
industrial settings (e.g., chem plant, chem spill.

+ Academics: Informal shorthand for a high school or university chemistry class or
major (e.g., studying for a chem exam). @ Cambridge Dictionary +4
