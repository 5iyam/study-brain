Organic chemistry is the scientific study of the structure, properties, composition,
reactions, and synthesis of carbon-based compounds. Because carbon forms
strong, stable covalent bonds with itself and other elements, it can generate a nearly
infinite variety of molecular structures essential to life, materials, and
pharmaceuticals. wikipedia +2

Major Subfields

* Biochemistry: The study of the chemical substances and vital processes that
occur within living organisms.

+ Medicinal Chemistry: The design, development, and synthesis of pharmaceutical
drugs and clinical treatments.

+ Polymer Chemistry: The creation of large, complex chain-like molecules
