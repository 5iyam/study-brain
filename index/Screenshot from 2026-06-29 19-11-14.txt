Biology is the scientific study of life and living organisms. It
explores everything from the microscopic mechanisms inside cells
to the complex interactions of entire ecosystems. By understanding
how organisms grow, function, evolve, and reproduce, biology
provides essential insights into ourselves and the natural

world. = wikipedia +2

Key Concepts in Biology

The discipline is typically unified by five core principles that explain all living
phenomena: = Wikipedia

* Cell Theory: All living things are made of cells, which are the basic units of life.

* Gene Theory: Traits and characteristics are inherited through genetic material
(DNA/RNA).

* Evolution: The process by which species adapt and change over generations.
+ Energy Processing: All living things require and transform energy to survive.

+ Homeostasis: The ability of organisms to maintain a stable internal environment
despite external changes.
